Weather Of The Island

The Fakarava Atoll and all of French Polynesia has a tropical, but moderate climate. There are two main seasons you will find here: a warm, rainy season from November to April, and a relativly cool dry season from May to October. The temperature varies only slightly throughout the year. At Papeete the average annual temperature is 79 °F (26 °C); the high average is 91 °F (33 °C) in March and the low average 70 °F (21 °C) in August. The Tubuai Islands, farther south, have a cooler climate; the low average can go down to 64 °F (18 °C) in September. The relative humidity is always high—generally between 80 and 90 percent. The more elevated areas are continually enveloped in heavy cloud formations. (Brittanica.com)
