Fun Things To Do

Snorkeling
Snorkeling is a great things to do when taking a vacation here. This is because of the beautiful ecosystem. The coral reef that the atoll is made of it in almost prime condition, unaffected by our garbage and carbon footprint. Also being in the middle of the Pacific it has a warm current coming in which creates a beautiful wildlife with any kind of marine widlife you could want. Fakarava has the highest concentration of grey reef sharks (not dangerous) in the world. So as long as you don't have a fear of sharks it can be quite a beautiful sight.
Explore the Town
The town of Rotoava is where you will find the airport and the majority of Fakarava's guest accommodations. There are a handful of pensions available around the atoll, including Havaiki Lodge, a guesthouse with a working pearl farm and a dazzling array of marine life just beyond its shores. Also Pension Raimiti, another guesthouse with beautiful, authentic architecture. The absence of any major resort presence only helps to maintain the atoll's pure surroundings and peaceful appeal.
Just Relax
Whether its by yourself or with your significant other, Fakarava has dozens of ways to just relax. Imagine biking around for an hour and having the road entirely to yourself, and then having fresh seafood for dinner every day before sleeping in your hammock to the sound of waves. Now tell me that doesn't sound like paradise. This island seems like it was made just to be a place to put you at peace.
